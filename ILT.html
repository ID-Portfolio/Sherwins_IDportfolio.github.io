The ILT page samples Facilitator Guides to illustrate the varied formats and communication styles developed for Instructor Led Triaining.

Sample Facilitator Guide, developed in theThrive LMS, was used to train T-Mobile's frontline sales staff and support call centers. The headings: Purpose, Explain, Instructions, and Ask explicitly provide instructors facilitating the activity the why, what, and how to deliver course activities.
